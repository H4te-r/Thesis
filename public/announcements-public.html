Emergency: Road Closure

Posted by Captain Maria Santos

Emergency Jan 15, 2024

URGENT NOTICE: Matino Street will be closed from January 16-20, 2024 for emergency road repairs. All residents are advised to use alternative routes during this period.

Alternative routes:
• Santos Street to Magsaysay Avenue
• Rizal Street to Quezon Avenue
• Dela Cruz Street to EDSA

For emergencies, please contact our hotline: (02) 8123-4569

Community Cleanup Drive

Posted by Secretary Noela V. Impreso

Event Jan 14, 2024

Join us for our monthly community cleanup drive on January 25, 2024 at 6:00 AM. Meet at the barangay hall for equipment distribution.

What to bring:
• Gloves and face masks
• Drinking water
• Positive attitude

Breakfast will be provided for all participants. Let's work together to keep our community clean!

New Certificate Processing Hours

Posted by Treasurer Maria Reyes

Service Jan 12, 2024

Starting January 20, 2024, our certificate processing hours will be updated to better serve our residents.

New Schedule:
• Monday - Friday: 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM
• Saturday: 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM
• Sunday: Closed

Online applications are still available 24/7 through our website.

Barangay Health Program

Posted by Dr. Sarah Lopez

General Jan 10, 2024

Free health screening will be available at the barangay hall every Wednesday from 8:00 AM to 12:00 PM.

Services include:
• Blood pressure monitoring
• Blood sugar testing
• Basic health consultation
• Vaccination updates

Please bring your barangay ID and health card. No appointment necessary.
