About Barangay Central

Barangay Central is a progressive community in Quezon City, committed to providing efficient and accessible government services to our residents.

Our Vision

To be a model barangay that provides excellent public service, promotes community development, and ensures the welfare of all residents through efficient governance and innovative solutions.

Our Mission

To deliver responsive, transparent, and accessible government services while fostering a safe, clean, and progressive community where every resident can thrive and contribute to our collective growth.
