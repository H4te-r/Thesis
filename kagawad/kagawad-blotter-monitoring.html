Blotter Filing Guidelines

When to File a Blotter

  • Criminal incidents (theft, assault, etc.)
  • Neighbor disputes requiring documentation
  • Property damage incidents
  • Public disturbance complaints

What to Prepare

  • Detailed description of the incident
  • Date, time, and location of incident
  • Names and contact info of involved parties
  • Any supporting evidence or witnesses
